Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for my interview?

Make sure you have all documented paystubs, proof of residency and income. Though these are not all required, the process will go a lot faster if you do have these. Sometimes, you can get approved right away.

How long will it take to get approved?

It's hard to say, because this can vary from case to case. But, the more clear documentation you can provide upfront, will allow you to get approved faster.

Do I qualify for calfresh as a low income college student?

If you have less than $100 in your bank account, recieve financial aid, pursuing an associates or bachelors degree, and unemployed - than yes, it's probable that you qualify.
